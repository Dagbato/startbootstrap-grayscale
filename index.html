About The Prospect Park Zoo

The Prospect Park Zoo is a 12-acre zoo located off Flatbush Avenue on the eastern side of Prospect Park, Brooklyn, New York City. The zoo is open 365 days a year, and the animals are on exhibit all year round.

Our current hours are Monday, Nov 7, 2016 - Friday, Mar 31, 2017 Daily 10:00 AM - 4:30 PM

Animals exhibited range from African Brush-Tailed Porcupines to Wreathed Hornbills.

The Prospect Park Zoo presently offers children's educational programs, is engaged in restoration of endangered species populations, runs a wildlife theater and reaches out to the local community through volunteer programs.
